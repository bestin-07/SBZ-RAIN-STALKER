Privacy Policy

Gemma Raus is a personal project by Bestin Antu. This page explains what data is processed and why.

What we collect

We collect only what's strictly needed to make the app work:

  • Push subscription endpoint — a browser-generated token used to deliver rain notifications to your device. It contains no name, email, or location. Stored in our database only if you tap the bell icon and allow notifications.

That's it. No account. No cookies. No analytics. No advertising trackers.

What we don't collect

  • Your GPS location — coordinates go directly from your browser to Open-Meteo (Switzerland). We never see them.
  • Usage data, page views, or behaviour tracking.
  • Device fingerprints, IP logs, or any identifying information beyond the push token.

What's stored in your browser

Two small items in localStorage — no personal data:

  • phrase_seed a random number that rotates the status messages so they feel personal to you.
  • ios_hint_dismissed remembers that you closed the iOS install hint.

These stay in your browser and never leave it.

Push notifications

If you enable notifications, your browser's push service (listed below) generates a unique endpoint URL and two encryption keys. We store this on our server to send you alerts when a dry window opens.

Your endpoint passes through one of:

  • Google FCM (Chrome / Android)
  • Mozilla Push Service (Firefox)
  • Apple APNs (Safari / iOS)

These third parties are involved because push notifications are a browser-level feature — we don't control which service your browser uses.

Legal basis (GDPR)

  • Weather data lookup: legitimate interest (Art. 6(1)(f)) — the entire purpose of the app.
  • Push notifications: consent (Art. 6(1)(a)) — you opted in by tapping the bell. You can withdraw at any time.

How long we keep it

Your push endpoint is kept until you unsubscribe (bell icon → tap again to disable). On unsubscribe it's deleted immediately. Expired or inactive subscriptions are also removed automatically by the push services.

Your rights

  • Withdraw consent — tap the bell icon to unsubscribe. Your endpoint is deleted immediately.
  • Access / erasure — email bestindroid@gmail.com and we'll confirm deletion.
  • Complaint — you can lodge a complaint with the Austrian data protection authority (dsb.gv.at).
